Jumbo's Evil Babies manual

Jumbo's Evil Babies is a 2D Shooter / Arcade game that is accessible for everyone.
To start the game, press B,you immediately see a enemy on your screen!
Shoot Baby demons using the space bar. Stop the game whenever you want with the esc key.
walk back and forward with the S and A button and even your scores are shown at the top of your screen.
